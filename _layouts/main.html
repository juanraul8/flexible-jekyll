Juan Raúl Padrón Griffe

Hola! I am currently looking for a PhD position. I am really enthusiastic about the intersection of realistic image synthesis, graphics-based vision and machine learning for the digital acquisition, representation and understanding of the visual world. I have just finished my MSc Informatics at the Technical University of Munich in July, 2020. During my Master studies I focused mostly on the Computer Graphics and Vision subjects, where I was advised by Prof. Matthias Niessner and Dr. Justus Thies at the Visual Computing lab. Previously, I obtained a B.Sc​. in ​Computer Science with a major in Computer Graphics ​from the Central University of Venezuela in 2015, where I was advised by Prof. Rhadamés Carmona and MSc Héctor Navarro at the Centre for Computer Graphics.
